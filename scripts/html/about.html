Hi, I'm Edison Xu.

Most of you probably know me through SurferGarage. This site is a more complete entry point. You can get to know me better here, and read more of my unfiltered thoughts.

At SurferGarage, you may see 30% of me. On social media, maybe 50%. Here, I will share 100% of my blogs, projects, thoughts, and experiments.

I will keep updating this site, improving its features, and trying out fun, useful, and meaningful things. Feel free to come back anytime.

Who am I? I have built things across many fields.

More precisely, I am a system builder and modeler. I studied Big Data and have worked across engineering, product, content, community, and startups. I like using systems thinking, data analysis, and modeling to understand the world.

Then I apply those ideas to startups, products, agents, content, and sometimes even cocktails.

To me, the most important thing is simple: create real value in the real world, and earn real feedback from users.

That is why I chose entrepreneurship.

Entrepreneurship is how adventurers embrace the world today.

At 19, I achieved two dreams in a row. I led my own football team to a championship. I also opened my own store.

After that, I chose something harder: to spend the next ten years building a great and enduring company. The next Google or ByteDance. I really admire their cultures. They build systems. They evolve across cycles.

And I am still growing.

Right now, I am building SurferGarage.

We interview early-stage tech founders aged 16 to 28. We help more people see paths beyond the traditional one. Our goal is to become the first place to understand Gen Z tech founders, and help them be seen by the world earlier.

The source of my creation is simple:

I want to bring the right people together, and make beautiful things happen.

That is why I started with communities, then moved into media. Now, in the age of AI, more exciting explorations are on the way.

I like humans.

Through media, bars, and communities, I have met people from many layers of the real world. Everyday users. Founders. Investors. Creators. Builders.

That is why my view of the world is human-centered.

Technology, engineering, and systems should not make the world colder. They should help people connect better, create better, and live better.

So I will say my mission again: Bring the right people together, and make beautiful things happen.

All right, here is the quick version:

I am Edison Xu, a founder creating value in the real world. I build startups, products, media, and AI-native organizations with systems, engineering, and modeling.

My dream is to build a truly great and enduring company in the next ten years. I also want to keep using technology to bring the right people together, and make beautiful things happen.

Always looking for partners and crew members who want to sail together for the next decade.

Who am I？如你所见，我 build 过很多不同领域的事物。更准确地说，我是一位系统构建者与建模者。得益于大数据专业背景，以及过去在工程、产品、内容、社区和创业中的实践，我擅长用系统工程、数据分析与建模的方式理解世界，并把这些思维应用到 startup、产品、Agent、content，甚至是发明一款鸡尾酒。

依我所见，重中之处是在真实世界创造价值，并从用户那里获得回报，所以我选择了创业。创业，是这个时代冒险者拥抱世界的方式。
